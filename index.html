We're exploring a simple but important question: why isn't growing environmental awareness in India translating into real, on-ground action? While more people today are aware of climate issues, hands-on participation still remains limited.

We're compiling a list of genuine, structured volunteering and internship opportunities in the environmental and conservation space across India. This form helps us understand what's missing. If you care about the environment and have ever thought about contributing your time or skills, your input will help shape better, more accessible opportunities.
